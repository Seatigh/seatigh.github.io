ABOUT ME
Álvaro Rubio Castellano
Video Game Programmer - Computer engineer
I have studied a Degree in Computer Engineering (AI speciality) at UC3M and a Master's Degree in Video Game Development (Programming specialty) at UCM. I've been motivated to study these degrees since 12 when I ask myself "how a football soccer player could move in a videogame when I move the joystick of my controller".

During the master's degree, I have developed, together with 13 other classmates from the different master's degrees (design, programming, art and marketing & production), Guayota, an adventures and puzzles video game set in Canarian mythology.

Obviously, I love video games and everything related to them (development, playing...). Otherwise, the development of new technology is a field that draws my attention, as well as the world of motor (airplanes, F1, motorcycles), cyber security and sports.
